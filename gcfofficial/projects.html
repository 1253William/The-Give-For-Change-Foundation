Over 30 Postpartum mothers rescued from hospital detention

On the 25th of August 2022, The Give for Change Foundation made their second year donation to the Ashaiman Hospital. Mrs Matilda Kumi Mensah (2nd in command for nursing) received a donation of medical, electrical and cleaning supplies along with an undisclosed amount of money on behalf of the Ashaiman maternity unit to support mothers owing arrears to the facility.

Dr Eric Akwatia, the municipal director of health at Ashaiman on behalf of the Municipal Chief Executive of Ashaiman Municipal Assembly Hon. Albert Boakye commended us for the initiative and went further to speak about Ashaiman being a densely populated community which needs urgent support aside government efforts,therefore individuals and philanthropic bodies should channel most of their attention to that community.

To Dr Abraham Boakye, we at the Give for Change Foundation acknowlegde you for your wise words, motivation, counsel and encouragement. The smiles on the faces of these fresh postpartum mothers who had just delivered with their babies beside them were just heart warming. The whole team went in to encourage, share the love of Jesus with them and we distributed some packed essential new-born baby kits including baby clothes, feeding bottles, baby pacifiers and diapers. The stronger mothers who had not yet delivered and were able to grace the occasion with their presence, they were not left out in this activity.
