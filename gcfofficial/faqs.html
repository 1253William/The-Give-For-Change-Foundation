Frequently Asked Questions (FAQs)

Here Are Some Answers To Your Unwavering Questions

How can I make an in-kind donation?

You can make your donation us through our account with Chime Pay or via MTN Mobile Money. Visit our donation page here to make your donate.

How secure is your online donation platform?

By Using Chime, your donation is secured and encrypted to get to us. You can as well make donation via our Mobile Payment Account.

I mistakenly donated more than I wanted to. How do I get a refund?

Ofcourse, refund of any donation is allowed. Send us a message to our support team with your unique enquiry here

What Percentage of my donation goes to the cause?

We prioritize our causes as a result we use 100% of your donation to serve our projects and activities.

How do I volunteer for your cause?

You can send us a message with your details here and you will receive feedback on joining our volunteering team.However you can volunteer through donations and supporting us with useful materials.

How can I donate to honor or remember someone?

You can donate any amount using Chime Pay or via our Mobile Payment transfer. The amount you donate will be a means to honor a loved one.

Do you accept donations of books, school supplies, clothes or other materials?

Yes we do. Kindly contact us to make your donation.

What types of transactions can I use to make a donation?

You can use Chime Pay for International donors and MTN Mobile Money to send money for your donation.

Do you need to have a Chime Account to make a donation?

Yes, You need to create a Chime account or download the app to make your donation.
